Robotics- Students have designed robotic solutions which include

-Freight Elevator

-Automated Car

-Automated Conveyor Belt System.

Students will also be demonstrating their Botball robotics equipment.

Gateway 1- Students have created Water bottle rockets using their knowledge of flight and Newton’s Laws of motion.

Gateway 2- Students have designed, built, and tested a toy for a child with Cerebral Palsy to use in Physical Therapy. Students will be demonstrating their toys and presenting their final prototype.

Gateway 3- Students have solved one of the following problems using a Microcontroller

-Safety Equipment for someone exercising at night.

-A public art installation

-A dispenser for humans or animals.
